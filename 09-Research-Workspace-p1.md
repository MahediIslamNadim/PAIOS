# Research Workspace Overview & Architecture

## Purpose

The Research Workspace provides a comprehensive environment for academic and technical research within PAIOS.

It manages research papers, literature reviews, citations, experiments, datasets, research journals, and idea management in a connected knowledge system.

---

# Scope

The Research Workspace covers

Research Papers & Document Management

Literature Review & Annotation

Citation Manager (BibTeX, Zotero integration)

Experiment Manager

Research Journal

Idea Vault

Dataset Manager

Research Analytics

AI Research Assistant

---

# Design Principles

Citation Connected

Every claim links to its source.

Reproducible

Experiments are documented for reproduction.

Organized

Papers, notes, and data are structured.

Discoverable

Everything is searchable and linked.

AI Enhanced

AI summarizes, connects, and recommends.

---

# Research Workspace Architecture

Presentation Layer

Research Dashboard, Paper Reader, Citation Viewer

Application Layer

Paper Manager, Literature Reviewer, Citation Manager, Experiment Manager, Journal, Idea Vault, Dataset Manager

AI Layer

AI Research Assistant, Paper Summarizer, Literature Connector, Idea Generator, Citation Recommender

Knowledge Layer

Research Knowledge Graph, Citation Network, Experiment Data, Research History

Data Layer

Papers, Citations, Experiments, Datasets, Notes, Ideas

---

# Core Components

Paper Manager

Organize, read, and annotate research papers.

Literature Reviewer

Systematic literature review workflow.

Citation Manager

BibTeX management, Zotero sync, citation generation.

Experiment Manager

Plan, execute, and document research experiments.

Research Journal

Daily/weekly research log.

Idea Vault

Capture and develop research ideas.

Dataset Manager

Organize and document research datasets.

Research Analytics

Track reading, writing, and publication progress.

AI Research Assistant

Summarize papers, find connections, suggest related work.
