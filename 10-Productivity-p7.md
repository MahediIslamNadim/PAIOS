# Productivity Analytics & AI Integration

## Purpose

Productivity Analytics provides data-driven insights into time usage, goal progress, and productivity patterns.

AI Integration brings intelligent planning, recommendations, and predictions to optimize daily productivity.

"Your peak focus is 9-11 AM. Schedule deep work then."

"Tuesday afternoons have low focus. Use for admin tasks."

"Your coding productivity increases after exercise."
